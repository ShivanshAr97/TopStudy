About

The company was launched in 2021. It primarily provides services to college students in various fields of Data Dcience, Website and App Development and DSA preparation in various languages along with interview preparations. Currently the company's primary business is live online tutoring in hindi and english It uses a White Board Video Environment (WVE) method for their 1-1 student teacher live sessions. In January 2022, we joined Byju's, Simplilearn, Unacademy, Vedantu and upGrad and became one of the founding members of IAMAI's India EdTech Consortium and in March 2022, it launched W.V.E 3.0, an interactive platform that aims to support Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning.
